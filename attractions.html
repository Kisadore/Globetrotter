Top Attractions

Khao Sok National Park

Khao Sok National Park is a stunning natural reserve in southern Thailand, renowned for its ancient rainforests, dramatic limestone mountains, and pristine Cheow Lan Lake, offering visitors a paradise for wildlife spotting, trekking, and kayaking.

Chiang Mai

Chiang Mai, located in northern Thailand, is a cultural and historical gem known for its ancient temples, vibrant night markets, and picturesque mountainous landscapes, providing a perfect blend of traditional and contemporary Thai experiences.

Krabi

Krabi, situated on Thailand's Andaman coast, is famous for its breathtaking beaches, crystal-clear waters, and impressive limestone cliffs, making it a top destination for rock climbing, snorkeling, and island hopping.

Wat Arun Ratchawararam Ratchawaramahawihan

Wat Arun, also known as the Temple of Dawn, is an iconic riverside temple in Bangkok, celebrated for its striking prang (spires) decorated with colorful porcelain, offering stunning views, especially at sunset.

The Grand Palace

The Grand Palace in Bangkok is a magnificent complex that served as the royal residence for generations and is home to the revered Emerald Buddha, showcasing intricate Thai architecture and rich cultural heritage.

Chatuchak Weekend Market

Chatuchak Weekend Market in Bangkok is one of the world's largest and most diverse markets, featuring thousands of stalls selling everything from clothing and antiques to street food and plants, making it a shopper's paradise.
